# Self-hosting

I finally gave in and bought one of those MiniPC's you can find from Aliexpress for cheap. I have been avoiding these because I read somewhere that some of these things are phoning home or never get firmware/BIOS upgrades. Anyway I really wanted something to tinker with and do my self-hosting on.
There is plenty of these MiniPCs sold second hand and I ended up with something called "Firebat AK2" for 120€ off.. I know that name is awful but I will not be looking at it that much when it is mostly in my dark closet. Surprisingly system came with Linux Mint installed, apparently previous owner had used it
to watch and record TV shows and such. I tried to ask why he did not need it anymore but did not get very clear answer. When I got home though I found out that Wifi connectivity was pretty spotty
